📋 Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids handles information when you use our mobile application. KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids is an educational game app designed for children, featuring alphabet learning, memory games, coloring activities, and number learning.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This policy explains what data is and is not collected, how the app works, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

🗄️ Data We Collect

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

❌ Names or email addresses ❌ Phone numbers ❌ Location data ❌ Camera or microphone input ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, AAID, MAC) ❌ Financial information ❌ Social media profiles ❌ Any data transmitted off the device

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The app stores the following data only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

DataPurposeStorage
Game progress (stars, scores, best times) Remember your achievements between sessions Device local storage

📢 Third-Party Services — Google AdMob

KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a service by Google LLC. Because KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids is a child-directed app, AdMob is configured with strict privacy settings:

✓ Child-directed treatment enabled ✓ Non-personalized ads only ✓ Android Advertising ID (AAID) disabled ✓ No interest-based advertising ✓ No remarketing or user profiling

AdMob may collect limited, non-personal technical data (such as general device type and approximate IP-derived country) solely for the purpose of serving a contextual, age-appropriate advertisement. This is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

👶 Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids is designed for use by children. We comply with:

COPPA (USA) GDPR (EU/EEA) Google Play Families Policy

Specifically:

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
  • We do not share children's data with any third party for commercial purposes.
  • Advertising is served in child-directed mode with all tracking and personalization disabled.
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content sharing exist in the app.
  • No account creation or login is required or available.
If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us at devdive96@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

🔗 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties, except as described above (Google AdMob's limited contextual ad serving).

The app contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, social login integrations, or any other data-collecting third-party libraries.

🔒 Data Security

All data generated by the app is stored locally on the user's device using standard Android secure storage (AsyncStorage and SQLite). No user data is transmitted over any network by our app.

We do not operate any servers or databases that store user information, eliminating the risk of server-side data breaches.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

  • Game progress and saved paintings are retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data via device settings.
  • We retain no data on our end because we collect none.
  • To delete all app data: uninstall KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids, or go to Settings → Apps → KidsPlay: Fun Games for Kids → Clear Data on your device.
